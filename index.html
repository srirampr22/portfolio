About

I am a passionate and driven Robotics Engineer currently pursuing my Master of Science in Robotics at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My academic journey began with a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering specializaing in Instrumentation. I am a highly motivated individual who is always eager to learn new things and I thrive on challenges to constantly set goals for myself. I am ambitious and driven by my ability to make an impact on the people around me. My expertise lies in the seamless integration of software and hardware components in robotic systems. My graduate coursework has deepened my knowledge in areas such as Localization, navigation, deep learning for perception, controls and motion planning. Professionally, I have been involved in cutting-edge research at FLUENT Robotics and the Biologically Inspired Robotics and Dynamical Systems Lab at the University of Michigan, where my work focuses on developing advanced perception and navigation systems for robots, In summary, my journey in robotics is fueled by a deep-seated enthusiasm for innovating at the intersection of hardware robustness and software intelligence. I am constantly driven by the challenge of creating systems that are not only efficient and intelligent but also intuitive and groundbreaking. I'm excited to explore new opportunities and collaborations in the field of robotics and AI.
