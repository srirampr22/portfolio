Syrma SGS (Internship)

Designed a concept for a 6-DOF arm-robot ultrasonic welding system aimed at enhancing high-volume production yield in a semi-automated RFID-tag production line. To bring this concept to life, I programmed a simulation using GAZEBO and ROS, depicting the arm-robot performing the welding action. Further advancing this project, I implemented 'teach and repeat' waypoint navigation to demonstrate the required actions on the assembly line. To evaluate the system's efficiency, I conducted tests comparing the yield from manual operation processes with the robot's automated process over a set time period. This testing revealed a remarkable potential increase in production yield, reaching up to 98.8%, showcasing the significant impact of automation in industrial settings.
